# UPI-Transaction
1.Steady Growth: UPI usage is stable with periodic peaks, showing strong adoption across all major cities.

2.Top Banks: ICICI, HDFC, SBI, and Axis are the main contributors.

3.Preferred Merchants: Zomato and Swiggy dominate due to frequent small-value payments.

4.Payment Success: Over 79% of transactions are successful — a good indicator of system reliability.

5.Digital Inclusion: Equal participation by gender and across multiple devices highlights growing digital literacy.

This Power BI dashboard effectively demonstrates UPI’s growth, city-wise distribution, and user engagement trends in 2024. It can help banks, fintech firms, and policymakers track performance, enhance customer experience, and identify opportunities for digital payment expansion.
